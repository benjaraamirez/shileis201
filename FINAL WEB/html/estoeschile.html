      Santiago y Sus Alrededores
      33.4489° S, 70.6693° W

      Santiago captivates with its assorted panoramas and its versatility. The Chilean capital fills with life all those that visit. Lose yourself in its streets to find original art galleries, innovative design stores and arts & crafts fairs, as well as restaurants, bars and coffee shops. And if you are a night owl, do not miss Barrio Bellavista's nightlife! Learn more about Chile in its many museums or visit the Central Market to taste exquisite products of the Chilean cuisine. If the outdoors is your thing, admire the capital from the surrounding hills to marvel at the views of Santiago. When shopping, do not miss the elegant Alonso de Córdoba neighborhood and the city's many modern shopping malls.
