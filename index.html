School Life

Smartness was chasing all of us, almost everyone was faster.

School was a great but unusual place, were the most extremes ideologies met. Friends and pranks were part of almost all senior year. Teachers were good, some loved us, some hated us. This institution tried to be good, even tho the director changed 4 times in a year, and more than 10 in 3 years. Academically could have been better, but they managed to make us all feel like a big family

  • Top 3 best teachers: Tio Sergio, Mabel Avila, Cristian Barroso
  • Greatest Prank: Yellow Soap + Voiding

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints

Fear God, Not Man.

Life in a religion is hard. Back in chile, I was the only member among my friends, classmates, and people in general. I was always "different". I never said bad words, or drink, or do stuff in sunday. This may sound a little lame, but all this was the best choice Ive ever did. The Gospel of Jesus Christ helped me go trhough the hardest moments of my life, but it also helped me to keep my focus in the important things. Life gets a little easier with God in your side

  • Favourite Scriptures: D&C 122: 7-8, Psalms 23
  • Favourite Elderly Sister: Sister Hope , Sister Correa
